Who am I?


I am a happily married, father of 2, living in Logan, Queensland (Australia).

I currently work for The Lottery Corporation (Australia) on the Draw Administration Team, with my role being mainly to conduct and process the Lottery Draws for Australia.

I am an avid, self-taught programmer in Visual Basic (for MS Excel and MS Access), Python and MQL4 (Programming language used for MetaTrader 4 Trading platforms). I am also learning HTML5.

I am a novice bee-keeper, with our first hive starting in November, 2022 (which we have now harvested over 12kg of honey since then!).

My further background is in the Security Industry, having been a Control Room Manager for Security Companies.

I love creating robots (programs) for Foreign Exchange (FOREX) trading, with my main hobby being trading the FOREX - followed closely by watching movies, reading, travelling and beekeeping.

I am a fast learner with a penchant for solving problems as I think inside the box (Doctor Who fan - the box is bigger on the inside!)
